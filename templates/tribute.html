But you can still send a drive-by-fix patch — no signup required. Here's how (works on any public repo):
  1. Click the Files tab.
  2. Browse the tree and click the file you want to edit.
  3. Hit the blue ✎ Edit / patch button at the top right of the file view.
  4. Make your change in the editor.
  5. Fill in your name, email, and a short reason. Click Send Anonymous Offering.
